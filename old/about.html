Hello,

I'm an interdisciplinary designer.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my works. I just received a Master's degree from Integrated Design program at Köln International School of Design. Prior to that, I was a UX engineer. My job was to conduct user research, investigate industrial accidents and proposed design strategies.

I'm curious and always eager to learn. I like to keep an eye out for human behavior in daily life. For me, every design project is a unique journey. My goal is to create a brilliant design that not only solves problems but also provides a delightful experience.

Outside of design, I'm a tennis enthusiast, a foodie traveler, a tech-savvy person.
