UniShelf

Product Design, Universal Design

There are shelves in all kinds of height in our home, but they may not be as convenient to some family members who are children, elders or handicapped people. It might be extremely hard or even danger for these family members to try to grab items from shelves which is too high.

Existing Products

The design of UniShelf gained inspiration from the structure of Ferris wheel and the spirit of universal design. With the UniShelf, people no longer have to struggle to grab the item they need. It will send the item directly to you.
