Musicloud

Background

Performing music is street musicians, wedding singers, and resident singers meal tickets. Consequently, they are more likely to invest in equipment than amateurs. Therefore, my partner and I set them as target users after several researches and analyses. We put them at the core and then used design thinking to design a product which meets their requirements.

Discovery

After observing and interviewing them, we realized that they were frustrated by the inconvenience of traditional musical score. For example, to secure the musical score or not is mutually contradictory. If they want to prevent their music from being blown away or curling up the pages while performing in an outdoor concert, they should hold musical scores with a clip or a holder. But this makes them have to pause the performance in order to flip pages. Therefore, musicloud embeds a motion sensor, which detect flip page gesture such as wave hand or shake head, to flip pages.

Moreover, musical score books are heavy and it is hard to find a particular song among the books in a short time, hence the performers usually bring the popular musical score. However, sometimes the audiences might request an uncommon song. In such situation, the performers can only regret that they did not bring enough musical score. In addition, the performers like to note down some useful information on the musical score, such as jokes, anecdotes movements. Time after time the musical score became wrinkled and unreadable. Furthermore, the performers need an extra light to read traditional musical score in the dark. This means they have to bring power supplies, which can be a hassle for them.

Advance Preparation Problem

  • musical score books are too heavy
  • hard to organize the musical score
  • sloppy notes make the musical score unreadable

Performing Problem

  • musical score cannot be held easily
  • cannot find musical score when audiences request songs
  • performance time is limited by the power supply

Result

For Performers:

Musicloud provides the performance statistics, based on the data collected from the Busker app, to the performers. They can easily see what is the most popular genre in a particular place or know who loves their performance. With these crucial information, they are able to prepare their new performance in a precise direction.

For Audience:

Most of the people in my country are reserved. We seldom reveal emotions in public. I noticed that the many parents offer up money to the kids to give to the performers. Therefore, the audience who used the Busker app can request a song or give a tip to the performer without being awkward. Additionally, the performer can realize who is the real audience.
