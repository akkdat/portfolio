Clicap

Product Design, Patent

Background

We always see a row of beverage bottles on shelves in convenience stores. I noticed that some people are used to drinking bottled beverages through a straw, but most of the time they cannot drink up the whole bottle. In this kind of situation, they will have to jam the straw into the bottle in order to close the cap. Hence, when they want to drink the beverage again, they need to pull out the straw by finger. This action contaminates the beverage and makes fingers dirty as well. Therefore, I designed a bottle cap which can allow a straw to clip onto it to solve such a problem.

Clicap was the most popular design in the class. Therefore, I decided to commercialize it. I filed patents in Taiwan and China and I am now discussing with a manufacturer about its launching.
