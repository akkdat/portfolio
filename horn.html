Horn

Product Design, UX Design

Background

Taiwan is a gourmet paradise and food is an important part of our culture; however, Taiwanese dietary habits alter a lot because Taiwan rapidly transformed from an agricultural country to a highly industrial one, therefore making Taiwan become an affluent society. As a result, people are fussy about what they eat and they expect a better dining experience when they go to the restaurant.

Discovery

In this project, I am advocated on making a new design for dinnerware of beef noodle restaurants (one of the representative cuisines in Taiwan). I carried out field research in beef noodle restaurants and observed the customers’ behavior, and then found that the spoons always slide into the bowl, annoying customers.

I also conducted intercept and scheduled interviews which, after that, allowed me to come to the realization that disposable dinnerware spoils customers’ dining experience. With this valuable research data, I designed a set of dinnerware which matches the delectable meal and fits the dining environment.

Result

The goal is to design a set of mixed materials dining ware, which creates visual consistency in the beef noodles restaurant.

I created a solution to the sliding problem using magnetic properties of attraction between a magnetic spoon and the bowl’s edge. Horn aims to give people a unique experience while enjoying a bowl of beef noodle.
